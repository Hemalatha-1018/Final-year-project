About SchemeBot

Discover how our AI-powered platform is revolutionizing access to government and financial schemes through intelligent, personalized recommendations.

Our Story

SchemeBot was born from a simple observation: millions of people miss out on beneficial government and financial schemes simply because they don't know they exist or find the application process too complex.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to bridge the information gap using artificial intelligence. We've built an intelligent system that understands your unique circumstances and matches you with the perfect schemes in seconds.

Today, SchemeBot serves over 500,000 users across multiple countries, helping them access billions in benefits they were eligible for but might have otherwise missed.

Our Technology

At the heart of SchemeBot is a sophisticated AI engine that combines natural language processing, machine learning, and comprehensive scheme databases.

Our system is trained on thousands of government documents, financial regulations, and scheme guidelines to ensure accurate, up-to-date information. The AI continuously learns from user interactions, improving its recommendations over time.

When you chat with SchemeBot, our NLP technology understands your queries in natural language, while our matching algorithms cross-reference your profile against eligibility criteria from thousands of schemes.
